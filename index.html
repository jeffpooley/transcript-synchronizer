How it works

  1. Upload your corrected transcript (PDF or TXT with speaker labels like "John:" or "Interviewer:")
  2. Upload your uncorrected VTT file (with timestamps from MacWhisper or similar)
  3. Click "Process" to align and merge them
  4. Download your new VTT file with corrected text and accurate timestamps
